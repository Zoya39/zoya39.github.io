ZOYA MATSIY - DATA ANALYST

Rockbuster Stealth LLC - Data Analysis

OVERVIEW

Rockbuster Stealth LLC, a movie rental company, is adapting to the changing industry by launching an online video rental service to compete with Netflix and Amazon Prime. The key objectives include determining movie contributions to revenue, average rental duration, customer locations, high lifetime value customers, and regional sales variations.

PROCESSING

Employed PostgreSQL to load Rockbuster's dataset and conducted thorough SQL analysis to extract insights on movie revenue, rental durations, customer demographics, high-value locations, and regional sales.

Presented findings through compelling data visualizations to facilitate informed decision-making and usage of SQL-based data analysis and effective communication of insights for strategic impact.

RECOMMRNDATIONS

  1. Targeting promotions in high-tension countries like India, China, the United States, Mexico, Brazil, and Japan, which contribute 39% of total sales.
  2. Prioritizing loyal customers who generate 20% of revenue, often from non-top customer volume countries.
  3. Promoting repeat orders in countries with high customer fidelity, where up to 80 repeat orders are possible.
  4. Focus on NC-17 and PG-13 film categories, which are top performers for sales.
  5. Noting successful competition between two stores with different sales strategies, suggesting potential collaboration for mutual enrichment.
