Instacart Grocery Basket Analysis

OVERVIEW

The project focused on providing customer behavior research based on sales descriptive data analysis to Instacart Co., an online grocery store. The objective was to uncover valuable insights into sales patterns, aiding in the formulation of a more targeted marketing and sales strategy.

PROCESSING

The client's database underwent thorough and bias-avoidant analysis using Python in Jupyter Notebooks, with data divided into 6 groups and multiple analyses conducted. Data handling included importation, cleaning, and quality checks. Various visualizations effectively communicated the findings, and the project's conclusion featured a comprehensive final report with professional insights and recommendations for Instacart stakeholders, including proper data citations.

INSIGHTS

Insight by product and geographical analysis:
The map presents the organic product sales distribution by state that has weak correspondence to total sales distribution. The pattern could be important in planning of marketing activity



Insight by general analysis:
The data indicates that the MLC (Mature Loyal Consumer) customer group represents 7% of the total customer base, and contributes to 22% of purchases and 28% of sales volume for the company.

RECOMMRNDATIONS

Evidenced pattern and recommendations summary:

  1. General Data Analysis: Focus on transitioning customers from regular to Loyal Customer groups. Importance of the Young Investors (YI) group creation.
  2. Demographic Analysis: Customers with 2 dependants drive most purchases; focus on increasing this customer segment across all groups.
  3. Product Analysis: Arizona State shows the highest potential for more important product lines.
  4. Behavior Analysis:
    - targeting Young Advanced Consumers and mature investors shows purchase peaks at 10 a.m. and 3 p.m.
    - Repetitive advertising, like reminders, would be effective for all groups.
  5. Geographic Analysis: Young Loyal Customers are the most vulnerable group.
    Texas, Washington, and Wisconsin are the top sales states across all groups.
