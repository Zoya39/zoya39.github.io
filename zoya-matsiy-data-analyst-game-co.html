ZOYA MATSIY - DATA ANALYST

Game Co. - Business Data Analysis

OVERVIEW

Data analysis for the game sailing company was conducted to define goals for annual marketing budget planning to maximize return on investment. The provided database included 3 regions’ sales statistics and global data. It was to define data inside to create a better understanding of the business dynamics.

PROCESSING

Utilizing Excel and embedded programming, I analyzed regional sales data. Stakeholder-provided development versions were scrutinized, revealing inconsistencies.

A descriptive analysis of historical sales identified strengths and weaknesses, informing carefully formulated recommendations.

INSIGHTS

RECOMMRNDATIONS

Recommendations for Marketing Support by Region:

  1. NA region: Increase marketing support for Role-Playing game sales, which currently stand at 46% of the previous year's sales.
  2. EU region: Provide stronger support for Role-Playing game and Action game sales, currently at 35% and 74% of the previous year's sales, respectively.
  3. JP region: Enhance marketing and management support for Role-Playing game and Fighting game sales, which have seen declines of 44% and 39% from the previous year, respectively.
  4. Other regions: Offer support for Role-Playing game and Music genre game sales, currently at 60% and 57% of the previous year, respectively. Additionally, consider revising the sales strategy.
  5. All regions: Conduct a comprehensive assortment revision to address declining sales across regions.
